Implement 8 new operations for full CRUD coverage of project files: list/modify/remove for lib tables, net classes, and design rules, plus modify_project_settings for .kicad_pro write support.

Output: 8 new operation schemas, 8 handler registrations in executor, 2 new methods on DesignRulesFile, 1 new function in project_file.py, all wired into schema.py union and __all__.
